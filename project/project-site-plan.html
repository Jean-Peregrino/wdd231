Site Name

Cajamarca Carnaval

The Cajamarca Carnival is a traditional celebration that takes place in northern Peru and is considered one of the most important festivals in the region. It is characterized by music, dance, contests, parades, gastronomy and popular dances.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Carnaval de Cajamarca website is to provide comprehensive information about the Cajamarca Carnival, the "Happiest Festival in Peru." The site aims to inform visitors about the festival's history, events, schedules, and how to participate in the celebrations. Additionally, the site serves as a guide for visitors, providing practical tips for planning their visit, including accommodation, travel information, and safety tips. A photo gallery showcases the vibrant atmosphere of the event.

Scenarios

  1. For Tourists: How can I plan my visit to the Cajamarca Carnival in 2024? What events and activities should I not miss?
  2. For Local Residents: What are the historical origins of the Cajamarca Carnival, and how has it evolved over time? What are the key traditions and cultural practices of the carnival?

Wireframe - Desktop and mobile view

Mobile View:

  1. Header: The page will display a header with the site title "Carnaval de Cajamarca" and a small logo. A hamburger menu will be provided to navigate through sections (Home, History and Origins, Event Schedule, Photo Gallery, Visitor Information, Contact).
  2. Main Content: The content area will feature sections such as an introductory paragraph about the carnival, key event highlights, and a gallery with images.
  3. Footer: A simplified footer with copyright and last modified information.

Wider View (Desktop):

  1. Header: The header will expand to show the site title, logo, and a horizontal navigation bar with all links clearly visible.
  2. Main Content: Sections will be displayed side-by-side for a more spacious layout. Each section (e.g., history, event schedule) will be prominently featured with accompanying images and descriptions.
  3. Footer: The footer will be at the bottom, containing the copyright and last updated details, with additional space for any extra information like social media links or contact details.

Project Rationale

This site plan outlines the structure, purpose, and design for the "Carnaval de Cajamarca" website. The goal is to provide users with an informative, engaging, and visually appealing platform to learn about the carnival and plan their visit. With a clear color scheme, font selection, and responsive design, the site will ensure a great user experience across devices.
